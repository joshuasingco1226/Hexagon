Basic Cybersecurity Tips

Stay safe online with these practical, everyday tips from Hexagon Corp.

Top 10 Cybersecurity Tips

  • 🔒 Use strong, unique passwords for each account.
  • 🧠 Never share your passwords with others.
  • 🔄 Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) whenever possible.
  • 📬 Be cautious of suspicious emails or links — they may be phishing attempts.
  • 🛑 Don’t download attachments from unknown sources.
  • 🔧 Keep your software and operating system up to date.
  • 🧹 Regularly back up your important files to a secure location.
  • 🌐 Use a secure network — avoid public Wi-Fi when accessing sensitive information.
  • 🛡️ Install and maintain trusted antivirus/anti-malware software.
  • 📱 Lock your devices and enable remote wipe features when available.
