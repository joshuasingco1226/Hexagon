Basic Cybersecurity Tips

Stay safe online with these practical, everyday tips from Hexagon Corp.

Top 10 Cybersecurity Tips

  • 🔒 Use strong, unique passwords for each account.
  • 🧠 Never share your passwords with others.
  • 🔄 Enable multifactor authentication (MFA) whenever possible, especially on your mobile devices.
  • 📬 Be cautious of suspicious emails or links — they may be phishing attempts.
  • 🛑 Always avoid downloading attachments from unknown sources.
  • 🔧 Keep your software and operating system up to date.
  • 🧹 Regularly back up your important files to a secure location.
  • 🌐 Use a secure network — avoid public Wi-Fi when accessing sensitive information.
  • 🛡️ Install and maintain trusted antivirus/anti-malware software.
  • 📱 Lock your devices and enable remote wipe features when available.

Top 10 Network Security Tips

  • 🧱 Use a firewall to monitor incoming and outgoing traffic.
  • 📶 Change default router settings, including SSID and admin passwords.
  • 🔐 Use WPA3 encryption for Wi-Fi when available; avoid WEP/WPA.
  • 📡 Disable unused ports and services on your network devices.
  • 👀 Monitor network traffic for unusual behavior or spikes in usage.
  • 🌐 Create separate guest Wi-Fi networks for visitors or IoT devices.
  • 🚫 Limit admin access to routers and switches — use role-based controls.
  • 📲 Keep firmware and router OS updated to patch vulnerabilities.
  • 🕵️‍♂️ Use VPNs for remote access to secure data in transit. Most applicable especially for remote worker contractors
  • ⚠️ Conduct regular network security audits and vulnerability scans.
