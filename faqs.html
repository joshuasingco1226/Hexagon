Frequently Asked Questions

Security Policies

Q: How does Hexagon Corp ensure data security?

A: We implement industry-standard encryption, multi-factor authentication, and regular security audits.

Q: What should I do if I suspect a security breach?

A: Immediately report it to the IT security team via the internal incident response system.

Network Policies

Q: Can I use personal devices on the company network?

A: Only if they meet security compliance standards and are registered with IT.

Q: Are employees allowed to use public Wi-Fi for work?

A: It is discouraged unless a VPN is used to ensure data encryption and secure access.

User Policies

Q: How often should I update my password?

A: Passwords should be changed every 90 days and must follow the company's complexity requirements.

Q: What are the rules regarding software installation?

A: Only IT-approved software is allowed on company systems to prevent security risks.
