Security Policies

Q: How does Hexagon Corp ensure data security?

A: We implement industry-standard encryption, multi-factor authentication, and regular security audits.

Q: What should I do if I suspect a security breach?

A: Immediately report it to the IT security team via the internal incident response system.

Q: Does Hexagon Corp provide security awareness training?

A: Yes, employees undergo regular cybersecurity training to recognize and mitigate potential threats.

Q: What encryption methods does Hexagon Corp use?

A: We use AES-256 encryption for data storage and TLS 1.2+ for secure communications.

Network Policies

Q: Can I use personal devices on the company network?

A: Only if they meet security compliance standards and are registered with IT.

Q: Are employees allowed to use public Wi-Fi for work?

A: It is discouraged unless a VPN is used to ensure data encryption and secure access.

Q: How does Hexagon Corp monitor network activity?

A: We use advanced monitoring tools to detect and prevent unauthorized access and security threats.

Q: What firewall protections are in place?

A: Our network uses multi-layered firewalls, intrusion detection systems, and real-time traffic monitoring.

User Policies

Q: How often should I update my password?

A: Passwords should be changed every 90 days and must follow the company's complexity requirements.

Q: What are the rules regarding software installation?

A: Only IT-approved software is allowed on company systems to prevent security risks.

Q: Can I share my login credentials with colleagues?

A: No, sharing login credentials is strictly prohibited to maintain security and accountability.

Q: What is Hexagon Corp's policy on remote work security?

A: Employees must use company-approved VPNs, enable MFA, and follow remote security best practices.
